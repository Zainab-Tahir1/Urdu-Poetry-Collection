Welcome to the World of Urdu Poetry

Urdu, a language of profound beauty, has played an essential role in shaping the literary and cultural landscape of the subcontinent, particularly in Pakistan. Rooted in Persian, Arabic, and local dialects, Urdu poetry transcends time and space, connecting people through shared emotions, wisdom, and artistry. With its rich metaphors and vivid expressions, Urdu poetry has deeply influenced Pakistan's literature, art, and music, embodying the cultural ethos of the nation.

Featured Poem of the Day

ہم کو ان سے وفا کی ہے امید
جو نہیں جانتے وفا کیا ہے

- مرزا غالب

"We hope for loyalty from them,
Who do not even know what loyalty means."
